# Split Wiser 🌍💸

An offline-first, mathematically-optimized group travel expense tracker explicitly built for seamless mobile use. No backend servers, no mandatory cloud accounts, and no complex configuration—just lightning-fast local logging and instant zero-friction debt settlement. 

## Features

- **Dynamic Dual-Currency Engine**: Log your expenses in any combination of Base or Local currencies. The app natively maps transactions out to both currencies side-by-side.
- **Live Local ForeX Sync**: Auto-fetches live exchange data seamlessly in the background and structurally "locks" the exact rate to the exact day the expense occurred, guaranteeing historical accuracy across long trips without disputes!
- **Smart GPS Geotags**: Tap the `📍` to instantly tag precisely where the expense occurred so you never forget where dinner was.
- **Instant Snapshot Sharing**: Don’t want people struggling with cloud accounts? Just tap "Share Image" to generate a high-fidelity Snapshot of your mathematically optimized Group Ledger, and directly AirDrop or WhatsApp it straight to your friends!
- **Data Export & Sovereignty**: Total device-level privacy. Cleanly export your transaction logs straight into standard `.csv` Excel spreadsheets whenever you wish, or violently wipe the entire memory bank directly from the device.

Built intelligently with Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS for extremely frictionless loading times over unstable international networks!
